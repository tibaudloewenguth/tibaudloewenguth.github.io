vestige

OVERVIEW:

The Vestige is a Japanese 90's inspired car. With 610 horsepower, produced with the help of two turbos, Epsilon has brought back the trending popup headlights. We have created a compact, 2 door car that screams the design of the 90's. A very high top speed of 305 km/h. Experience the thrilling boost of the turbos. Furthermore, enjoy the rolling anti lag system. Charge up the boost and witness a surge of acceleration as the car lurches forwards. A retro red and black interior transports you to the 90's. To top it all off, the modification potential on the Vestige is unlimited.

twin turbos
hear the whistle

Additional to the engine, two turbos have been tuned to perfection. Listen to the magnificent spooling get louder the more you accelerate. Topped with the satisfying flutter as you take your foot off the gas. Turbos work by compressing more oxygen into the engine's cylinders. While you increase speed, the boost charges up. Feel the boost of the turbos kick in once you pass 3000 RPM.

launch while moving

Anti lag is a system used to prepare the turbo boost. Before reaching the boost RPM and the gradual kick of the turbos, the turbos can be spooled. A button on the steering wheel is used to perform rolling anti lag. Hold the button and listen to the turbos charge manually. After 2 seconds, take your finger off. Then feel the acceleration as the car launches while rolling.

jdm culture
retro 90's

JDM (Japanese Distributed Market) cars are famous for their capabilities to be modified. With cars from the 90's, modifications to aesthetics and performance are common. Epsilon suggests against modifying the engine of the Vestige. However, we strongly encourage you to make the car your own. Each car's shell is constructed slightly differently. The chassis of the car can be easily dismantled with the correct tools. Providing opportunities to apply body kits or swap out parts. The possibilities are endless.
