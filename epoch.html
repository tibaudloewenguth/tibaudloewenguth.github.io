epoch

OVERVIEW:

The Epoch provides a comfortable cruise. The raw sound of the naturally aspirated V6 is the sound of perfection. Emphasising the experience, the engine block is hollowed out and connected to the interior of the car. This allows you to feel the motion of the engine while driving. The design, inspired by Datsun boasts the look of the 1960s-70s Japanese era. The impressive 340 horsepower coming from 6 cylinders partners with the low weight of the car to provide an extreme power to weight ratio. Although not being the most powerful car, it's low weight makes up for it. Hence creating a unique, fast and raw driving experience.

feel the engine
natural sound system

Feel and hear every response of the car while you control it. The engine's sound system, inspired by Lexus' LFA is connected to the interior of the car. The hollowed out engine cap absorbs the vibrations of the motor, then passes it through pipes to transport the sound into the interior. The experience of driving the Epoch is enhanced through the connection with the car.

shift satisfaction

All Epochs are built as vehicles with manual transmissions. What stands out about the Epoch is the driving experience. The gear shifting system has been designed to perfection. Enjoy every shift as the car calibrates the revs for you every time. Without taking away the experience of driving a manual car, the automatic rev matching ensures smooth driving. The system provides a beautiful sound and feeling after ever shift. Designed for you to feel the kick of every gear, and to enjoy the experience.

travel through time
take a seat in the 60's

Leather seats, chrome silver machinery and varnished wood transports you back to the 1960's. But, comfortable and roomy as a modern car. Analogue dials and gauges are used to provide an authentic feel. Enjoy the design and looks of the past as a passenger or a driver.

driving on clouds

Vintage cars are known for their suspension, providing a floating sensation. This has been recreated artificially as a layer over the modern suspension. The Epoch sits on two levels of suspension. The first absorbs all the shock from the four wheels independently. The second manipulates the car to lean and bounce like a true 20th century car. Almost like driving a boat, the suspension system is sure to bring a smile to your face.
