OVERVIEW

The Rapide is a car with the perfect blend of comfort and speed. Powered by a naturally aspirated V10 exerting 700 horsepower. The design has been mastered to maximise downforce. High downforce weight means the car can hug the road. This provides better traction and handling around corners. The two seater sports car's transmission is done with paddle shifts. Have control over the gears while not worrying about using a clutch. Comfortably switch up and down the 7 gears using the paddles behind the wheel.

built with carbon
strong and light

Carbon fibre is one of the best materials for lightweight production, being half the weight of aluminium and up to 5 times stronger. It helps keep the chassis strong and light. We produce our own carbon fibre at Epsilon to use on our vehicles. Resulting in high performance, increased strength and better power to weight ratios. Carbon fibre is known for its intense fire resistance. In an accident, the carbon withstands up to 600 degrees Celsius, being a safety feature of the Rapide.

carbon plates

The plating of the car is also built with carbon fibre. This includes the bottom of the seats, the dashboard, and the roof and hood. Extra carbon parts are available to swap for the areas using aluminium. The Rapide's interior is designed with top quality exposed carbon fibre, providing a unique pattern.
