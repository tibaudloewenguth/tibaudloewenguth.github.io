building the beauty
advanced engineering

Epsilon Motors are proud to display the pinnacle of engineering in our current time. The engineering of our vehicles has been perfected. Achieved through consistent testing on the track in various climates around the world. This ensures that our cars are suitable for anyone, anywhere. Epsilon has taken a new approach to resourceful production. We ethically produce our vehicles through environmentally friendly processes.

artistic designs

The team creating the elegant builds is comprised of 3D designers and aerodynamic engineers. Using advanced physics simulations, they ensure the designs are both efficient and beautiful. Producing some of the most visually appealing vehicles, seeming either classic or modern.

who are we?
enthusiasts

We are enthusiasts who want to share our passion for automobiles with the rest of the world. Epsilon wants to create vehicles that car enthusiasts want. By maximising our connections with the public, we can reflect the desires of car enthusiasts and create cars that people love.

nostalgic

Epsilon wants to bring back the golden era of automobiles. Revolutionising vehicle design, we aim to showcase the nostalgic looks of the 20th century in our modern cars. Presented through our Timeless models, we conserve the history of automobiles. We believe the rising trend of electric motors takes away the soul of sophisticated cars.

upcoming events
August 8th, 2024 - voting

The triannual voting event allows you to contribute to the creation of a new car. Through social media, everyone has a chance to vote on the next vehicle. The voting is split into two parts: Exterior + Design and Engine + Mechanics. The voting period is over two weeks, beginning on the 8th of August 2024. Epsilon is glad to connect with the community by sharing our passion with you.

January 3rd 2025 - launch

A new line of vehicles, built for comfort. Epsilon plans on releasing three new vehicles under a unique category. Focusing on engineering suspension and support instead of speed and efficiency. Release to the public planned at the start of 2025 to celebrate the new year. Come witness the unveiling of our three vehicles at the Epsilon HQ in Melbourne, Australia on the 3rd of January. The event will include a car show, displaying the best of our work, with the reveal of the new vehicles at 2pm AEST.

monthly meets
about

Monthly Meets is a program run by Epsilon. Bring your works of art to a destination determined by us, all cars are welcome. We would love to see your builds and meet new people with similar interests. This is an opportunity to meet like-minded people and see amazing cars. The meets are hosted on the first Sunday of every month. Running from 9am - 2pm. Complementary food and drinks provided. Come meet the team at our next meet.
