le mans survivor

Epsilon's Le Mans cars are vehicles that have completed the French 24 hour race. An entire day and night of non-stop racing under extreme circumstances. Built to handle pressure and demonstrate the epitome of endurance racing. Epsilon's Le Mans cars compete in the LMH class (Le Mans Hypercar). The cars for sale are survivors of the extreme conditions of endurance racing.

authenticity

Our Le Mans cars are tested for faults after racing. This is to ensure that the vehicles are functional and safe after enduring the violent demands of endurance racing. The car's body is kept untouched, unless structural damage is present. We keep the scars and scratches from racing under such extreme conditions on the car. This keeps the vehicle authentic and preserves its history.
