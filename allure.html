allure

OVERVIEW:

The Allure is a true speed demon. The combustion engine paired with the electric motors provide a staggering 860 horsepower. The hybrid system results in 790 Nm of torque. A top speed of 405 Km/h. One of the fastest production cars to date. Multiple engine usage systems are available. Choose to use only electric power, combustion, or both. The low stance of the car minimises air resistance and provides high downforce. Abundant use of carbon fibre keeps the weight of the car low. This compensates for the weight of the electric motors.

hybrid power
fast acceleration

The two electric motors powering the rear wheels provide most of the torque. Electric motors are known for their powerful acceleration. Fly off the start line with the 790 Nm of torque. Incredibly fast acceleration from the hybrid system gives a thrill to the passengers.

electric to combustion

After reaching 20 km/h, the power from electric motors will cease. The engine's power will transfer to the combustion system. Listen as the quiet whirring electric motors introduce the screaming V12. Starting with a rip to calibrate revs, the sound of the switch is music to the ears. Manual controls are available to choose whether to use only electric or combustion systems. This is designed in the event of fuel or batteries running low. We recommend using the hybrid system unless a change is necessary.

track racing 
light handling

Take turns in the Allure with high speeds. The light car allows for high handling capabilities. Accelerate out of a turn instantly with the help of the hybrid engine. The control of the car feels simple and reliable, comfortable turning at high speeds with the low build and high downforce.

advanced A.B.S

Start slowing down later before turns with the advanced ABS system. Anti Lock Breaking is a system that prevents skidding when slowing down. The ABS system on the Allure is the creation of impressive engineering. The ceramic brakes result in no dust while braking. This means keeping the mechanics clean and durable. Perfect for track use, and higher safety.
