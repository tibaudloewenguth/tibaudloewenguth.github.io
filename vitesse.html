all wheel drive
Take corners with speed

Epsilon's Vitesse does not use downforce as the main contributor to taking corners. The innovative all wheel drive system replaces the need for high downforce. Inspired by Nissan's E-TS system, the power delivered to each wheel differs. When cornering, the 2 parallel drive trains split the power of the engine. A ratio is then calculated to find the optimal power for each wheel. This split power capability allows for the car to smoothly and quickly take corners. A true innovation in the automotive industry.

maximum traction and speed

To avoid tyres spinning on a launch, the Vitesse provides power to all four wheels. However, this distribution of power results in a lower top speed. To conserve the power of the engine, the switch to rear wheel drive is casual. After the launch, the power ratio gradually shifts to the rear wheels. Resulting in a high top speed, while not losing the launching ability of all wheel drive vehicles.
