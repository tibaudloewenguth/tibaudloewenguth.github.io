gtr-4

OVERVIEW:

The GTR-4 is the 4th generation of Epsilon's GT race cars. The successor to the 2nd and 3rd gen GT cars, which are now discontinued. The GTR-4 races in Gran Touring races around the world. The production GTR-4 cars have different interior components to the cars used in racing. The steering wheel and seats are more comfortable and accessible compared to conventional race cars. Built for track use, the GTR-4 excels in top speed over long straights. Achieved with the help of the ambitious quad turbo induction system.

record speed 
top speed record

The GTR-4 holds the highest top speed of any GT production car. With power to weight regulations set by the FIA, top speeds over 350km/h are difficult to achieve. The first quad turbo system applied in GT racing results in the GTR-4 pushing to 390km/h. Epsilon has set a new world record for the top speed potential of a GT car.

aeronautical engineering

The GTR-4 uses mechanics inspired by planes to reduce speeds quickly. Deceleration systems from aeroplane engineering are applied to the vehicle. Extending flaps and the rear wing creates more drag and helps slow down the car. This allows for the GTR-4 to push its strength of top speed while comfortably being able to slow down before a turn.
